Uncle Quan's Vegan Lo Mein

You won't even miss the meat in this melange of oriental sauces, veggies and noodles

  • 1 lb Chinese Wheat Noodles
  • 3 TBSP Light Soy Sauce
  • 1 TBSP Mushroom Oyster Sauce
  • 1 TBSP Dark Soy Sauce
  • 2 TBSP Sugar
  • 1 tsp MSG
  • 1 TBSP Cornstarch
  • 4 TBSP Neutral Cooking Oil
  • 16oz Mushrooms; Sliced
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic; Minced
  • 1 Small Knob of Peeled Ginger
  • 1 White Onion; Sliced
  • 4 Stalks of Green Onions; Whites and Greens cut into 1 inch segments
  • 8oz Sugar Snap Peas
  • 1 Bell Pepper; Sliced
  1. Mix together, Light and dark soy sauce, mushroom oyster sauce, sugar, cornstarch and 1 Tbsp water
  2. Boil noodles according to package directions
  3. Preheat wok on stove on highest heat possible
  4. Add oil to smoking Wok and swirl around
  5. Add Mushrooms and cook until moisture is gone
  6. Add onion and bell pepper and cook for 1 minute
  7. Add Sugar snap peas, ginger and garlic
  8. Once fragrant, push veggies aside, add more oil and add in cooked noodles
  9. Toss noodles with veggies until thoroughly mixed
  10. Add in sauce mixture and keep tossing
  11. Add in green onions, sesame oil and MSG
  12. Remove wok from heat and serve immediately
