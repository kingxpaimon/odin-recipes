LASAGNA THAT WOULD MAKE GARFIELD NUT

This lasagna is guaranteed to blow your socks off. Garfield was locked up for 6 years because of this lasagna. The layers of pasta, bolognese, and various italian cheeses would put anybody to bed

  • dried lasagna noodles
  • 32 oz can of San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 lb of ground beef or vegan meat substitute
  • 16oz of Ricotta Cheese
  • Kraft Green can Shake Cheese
  • Full fat brick of mozarella
  • 1 yellow onion; diced
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 1 cup of Fresh Basil LEAVES
  • Olive Oil
  1. Preheat the oven to 450F
  2. Start boiling a big pot of salted water
  3. Mix together ricotta, parmesan and shredded mozzarella
  4. In a sauce pan, heat up 3 tablespoons of Olive Oil over medium low heat
  5. Once the oil is shimmering, add your diced onion
  6. After a few minutes once the onions turn translucent and fragrant, add minced garlic
  7. Once the garlic is fragrant and before it burns, add the ground meat/vegan mince
  8. Keep moving the beef mixture around to brown
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  10. Your water should be boiling by now. Go ahead and add the dried lasagna noodles. Cook them until they are no longer rigid
  11. Once the noodles are cooked, drain in a colander
  12. In a casserole dish, build your layers starting with tomato sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, basil leaves and repeat until dish is filled
  13. Cover with aluminum foil and put in the oven for 45 minutes to an hour or until the cheese is bubling
